About The ChitraHarsha

ChitraHarsha (चित्रहर्ष), derived from Sanskrit, means "wonderful joy." Our mission is to bring that joy into every home through inclusive, engaging, and thoughtful play.

Founded on the principles of equality and inspired by India's rich heritage of storytelling and intellectual games, we believe play is a powerful tool. It can break down stereotypes, foster collaboration, and build empathy. We are committed to creating a safe, ad-free, and culturally-rooted digital playground for the next generation of thinkers, creators, and leaders, all while adhering to the highest standards of data privacy and security under Indian law.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 17 October 2025
